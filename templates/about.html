Election Campaign Analysis &
ML Prediction Dashboard

This project analyzes election data of 10 major Indian states across the years 2009, 2014, 2019 and 2024. It provides deep insights into voting patterns, party performance, campaign expenditure and voter behavior.

The dashboard also uses Machine Learning to predict the winning party in a constituency based on various factors.
